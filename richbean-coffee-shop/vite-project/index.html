About Richbean

Our story of coffee excellence

Founded in 2018 in the heart of Cape Town, Richbean began as a dream to create the perfect coffee experience. We source our beans directly from small farms across Africa, ensuring fair trade and exceptional quality in every cup.

Our master roasters craft each blend with precision and passion, bringing out the unique flavors that make each origin special. From the high altitudes of Ethiopian highlands to the rich volcanic soils of Kenya, every cup tells a story of its journey.

At Richbean, we believe coffee is more than a beverage—it's a moment of connection, a pause in your day, and a celebration of craftsmanship. Join us in experiencing coffee the way it was meant to be.
